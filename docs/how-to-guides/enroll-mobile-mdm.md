# How to Enroll Your Mobile Device in MDM (Microsoft Endpoint Manager)

## Overview

If you do not enroll your mobile device with Microsoft Endpoint Manager (MEM), formerly named Intune, you will not be able to access company email or data on your mobile device.

> **Important Note:** Enrolling your phone will **not** give the company the ability to wipe your personal data, control your phone, or retrieve your phone number. It creates a "sandbox" on your phone where apps and accounts held in that sandbox can securely interact with company email and cloud data.

The MEM Mobile Device Management (MDM) solution will:
- Prevent you from copying company data outside of the sandbox of managed apps on your device.
- Require you to install and use approved apps to read email (Outlook mobile app) and interact with data (OneDrive and SharePoint).

## After Enrollment: Installing Required Apps

After completing enrollment and installing the Outlook app from the Company Portal, you will also need to install the **Contacts App** so you can sync your contacts back to your phone.

### Enabling Contact Syncing in Outlook (iPhone)

1. Open the **Outlook app** on your iPhone.
2. Tap your **profile icon** in the top-left corner, then tap **Settings** (the gear icon).
3. Select your **Outlook account**.
4. Next to **"Save Contacts"**, toggle the slider to the **ON** position.
5. You'll receive a message asking if you'd like to save your Outlook contacts to your iPhone. Ensure your iPhone is plugged into power, then tap **"Save to my iPhone"**.

> **Note:** If you open a link to a Zoom or Teams meeting, you'll need to install that software from the Company Portal app after you enroll.
