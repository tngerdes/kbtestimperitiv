# Security Policy #4: Sanction Policy

## Scope of Policy

This policy governs employee sanctions and disciplinary actions for The Company. **All employees must comply with this policy.** Demonstrated competence in the requirements of this policy is an important part of the responsibilities of every employee.

---

## Policy Statement

It is the policy of The Company to establish and implement appropriate, fair, and consistent sanctions for employees who fail to follow established policies and procedures or who commit various offenses.

Sanctions applied shall be:
- Appropriate to the nature and severity of the error or offense.
- Applied on an **escalating scale**, with less severe sanctions for less severe offenses and more severe sanctions for more severe offenses.

Offenses involving obvious illegal activity may result in notifications to appropriate law enforcement authorities.

**All employee sanctions will be documented.**

---

## Levels of Disciplinary Action

Based on the severity of the violation, disciplinary action may include:

1. Verbal warning
2. Written warning
3. Education — training/retraining
4. Removal of system privileges
5. Suspension without pay
6. Termination of employment

---

## Procedures

- **Inadvertent release of PII and sensitive company data** — Will be investigated; punishment will be determined by management and the extent of harm to the individual involved.

- **Unauthorized access to PII/sensitive data** — Employees accessing PII and sensitive company data files that they do not have a reason to access is a violation that **may result in immediate termination**.

- **Blatant disregard for policies and procedures** — May result in **immediate termination**.

- **Intentional release of PII/sensitive data** — Intentional release of PII and sensitive company data to someone who should not have access to the information **WILL result in immediate termination and possible prosecution**.
