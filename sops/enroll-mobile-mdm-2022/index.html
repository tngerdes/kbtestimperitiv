    SOP: Mobile Device Enrollment in MDM (2022)

    Overview

    If you do not enroll your mobile device with Microsoft Endpoint Manager (MEM), formerly named Intune, you will not be able to access company email or data on your mobile device.

    Important Note: Enrolling your phone will not give the company the ability to wipe your personal data, control your phone, or retrieve your phone number. It creates a "sandbox" on your phone where apps and accounts held in that sandbox can securely interact with company email and cloud data.

    The MEM Mobile Device Management (MDM) solution will: - Prevent you from copying company data outside of the sandbox of managed apps on your device. - Require you to install and use approved apps to read email (Outlook mobile app) and interact with data (OneDrive, SharePoint).

    Prerequisites

    Before beginning enrollment:

    • If you already have your company email account on your phone, remove it first. Directions for removing account
    • Install the Company Portal App from your appropriate App Store. Read through the enrollment directions before setting it up or logging in.
    • Read the full directions and watch the relevant video before executing the steps.
